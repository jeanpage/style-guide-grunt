Panels

Use panels to contain side-bar content or secondary/tertiary content that is related but does not directly serve the same purpose as the primary content.

  • Use for Left Navs
  • Use for Learning Center content on the eCommerce site
  • Use for eCommerce content on the Learning Center
  • Typically panel content should not be full width or exceed more than half of the available columns
